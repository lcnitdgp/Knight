About NITMUN


A MUN: What is it?


"The people that are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do. "

MUNs are a facility to this dream, as we try to solve global problems, keeping diplomacy, international relations and other constraints in mind. In MUNs or Model United Nations, students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees to debate the organisations's various agendas.

NITMUN-VIII


NITMUN-VIII aims to bring a beacon of light into the global forum, where young minds attempt at finding solutions to real world problems, tackling global crises and suggesting alternate solutions to global decisions. NITMUN- VIII is all about challenging the status quo, the norm and cooperating to create a better future.


Been to an MUN before? Great! We look forward to seeing you at this one. Haven't been to one yet? No worries. Join us and fall in love with MUNinng!
